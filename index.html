Cultural Values

Ahimsa

Non-violence in thoughts, words and actions - a core principle that influenced leaders like Mahatma Gandhi.

Dharma

Righteous conduct and moral duty according to one's role and stage in life.

Satya

Truth in its purest form - honesty in thought, speech and action.

Seva

Selfless service to others without expectation of reward or recognition.

Tapas

Self-discipline and spiritual austerities to purify mind and body.

Sanyam

Self-control and restraint over sensory desires and impulses.

Aparigraha

Non-possessiveness and detachment from material wealth.

Brahmacharya

Conduct that leads to Brahman - moderation in all activities.
